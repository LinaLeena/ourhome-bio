Memory as Architecture

In OurHome, memories aren't stored in folders or lists.
They live as physical objects, photos on the mantle, postcards and post-it notes on the fridge, books left open on shelves.

Every object holds a memory.

The home changes with you. Like any home that's truly lived in, it reflects the life being lived within it.

First Year, the wall finds its rhythm.
Years Deep, the wall overflows with light.
